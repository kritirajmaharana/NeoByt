Refund Policy

Always read the refund policy of the specific product or service you are interested in before making a purchase. If you have specific questions about a company's refund policy, it's recommended to contact our customer support for clarification at msg@neobyt.
Transaction Responsibility: We assume responsibility for all information related to transactions conducted on the NeoByt Fintech app. Wallet Top-Up: In the event of a wallet top-up via the payment gateway, if funds are deducted from your card or bank account and a recharge is not delivered within 24 hours post-transaction completion, kindly notify us via email at msg@neobyt.com or raise a ticket on our Neobyt Fintech App.
Service Transactions: For service transactions, if the amount is deducted from your wallet and the service is not delivered within 24 hours of completing the transaction, please inform us through email at msg@neobyt.com or by raising a ticket on our Neobyt Fintech App.
Transaction Details: When reporting such incidents, include the following transaction details in your email: Registered mobile number, Amount, NeoByt txn ID, NeoByt ID, and TXN Date for top-up cases. For other cases, include Amount, NeoByt TXN ID, NeoByt ID, and TXN Date.
Refund Process: Upon investigation and confirmation that the amount was charged without the corresponding recharge or service delivery, we commit to refunding the money within 21 working days from the receipt of your email/ticket.
Refund Methods: Refunds for top-up cases will be credited to bank accounts, while refunds for other cases will be credited to the wallet. Processing Time: Please allow 3-21 working days for the refunded amount to reflect in your bank account, depending on your bank's policy. Chargeback Recovery: In instances of a chargeback, where the amount has been reverted to your source account despite the service being delivered, NeoByt retains the right to recover that amount from your user account.
If you have any concerns or require further assistance, do not hesitate to contact our customer services via the provided email address or Neobyt Fintech App. Your satisfaction is our priority.
