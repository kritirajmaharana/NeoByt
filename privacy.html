Privacy

At neobyt, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers, website visitors, and other stakeholders. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal information that we collect from you.




Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:




  • Contact information, such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Payment information, such as credit card number and billing address.
  • Demographic information, such as age, gender, and location.
  • Website usage information, such as IP address, browser type, and referring/exit pages.



We may collect this information through various means, including online forms, cookies, and other tracking technologies.




How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide products or services to you.
  • To process and fulfill orders.
  • To communicate with you about your orders or other requests.
  • To personalize your experience on our website.
  • To improve our products, services, and website.
  • To send you marketing communications, with your consent.
  • To comply with legal or regulatory requirements.



Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we hold. You may also have the right to object to certain uses of your information or to request that we restrict processing of your information. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.




Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at message@neobyt.com.




We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website or by other means.
